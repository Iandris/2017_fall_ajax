Descripion:

Sticky is a jquery plug in that allows you to identify individual items on your webpage, and lock its position to the top. For example, you can mark an item 1/2 way down your page as "sticky" and as you scroll it will hit the top of the page, and stay visible instead of scrolling out of view.

Example: See the div#header, div#button, div#another elements

Descripion: Shuffle Letters effect creates a jumbled line of text as it adds words to the page. the final message is unknown until the last letter of each word is added. A 1 second pause is added to the page load and the enter key press. after you enter some text, the text is reproduced above the input box one letter at a time, with each letter being replaced by a random letter or number. once each word is complete, the whole word is converted from the shuffled text back to the origional. This is repeated until the entire text is completed.
